## Major Discovery 1

Official Card Type / Insert Recognition Is Now The Main Bottleneck

Subset D shows the system often knows:

- Year
- Product
- Player
- Grade
- Serial

but still needs stronger knowledge of official card type / insert names.

Examples:

- Planet Earth
- Game Floor
- Apprentice Ink
- Dual Jerseys Prime
- Dual Patch
- Kaleidoscopic

These should be treated as publisher-defined card type or insert names, not generic descriptions.

## Major Discovery 2

Registry Expansion Has The Highest ROI

Priority registry candidates from Subset D:

- Planet Earth
- Game Floor
- Apprentice Ink
- Dual Jerseys Prime
- Dual Patch
- Kaleidoscopic

These should be protected once identified.

## Major Discovery 3

Parallel Precision Still Needs Work

Recurring parallel confusion:

- Black
- Gold
- Black Gold
- Gold Vinyl
- Orange Raywave
- Red Label

Parallel detection should avoid collapsing specific parallels into generic colors or dropping visible parallel terms.

## Major Discovery 4

RC Preservation Continues To Matter

The repeated Apprentice Ink cases show that RC can appear alongside official card type and auto.

RC should remain visible when evidence supports it.
