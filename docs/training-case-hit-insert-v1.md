# Listing Copilot Training Note - Case-Hit Insert Terminology (V1)

## Purpose

Subset B follow-up identified that high-value case-hit and insert terminology must not be dropped from listing titles.

These terms often drive buyer search behavior and pricing. They should be treated as high-value insert / SSP terminology, not ordinary parallel language.

## Initial Coverage

- Kaboom
- Ultraviolet
- Downtown
- Color Blast
- Stained Glass
- Manga
- Galactic
- Blank Slate
- Night Moves
- Permit to Dominate
- Net Marvels
- Aurora
- In Motion
- Micro Mosaic
- Zebra
- Tiger
- Elephant
- Gold Vinyl
- Black Pandora
- Genesis

## Rules

If the card front or back explicitly shows one of these design or insert names, the title must preserve it.

Do not treat these terms as generic parallels.

Do not force these terms from weak visual guesses. Use them only with text evidence or unmistakable design evidence.

If the insert name is visible but exact checklist taxonomy remains uncertain, confidence should usually be MEDIUM, not HIGH.

If the title omits a clearly visible term such as Kaboom, Ultraviolet, or Downtown, confidence must be downgraded.
